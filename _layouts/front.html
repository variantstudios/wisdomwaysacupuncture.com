Specializing in Acupuncture for Women's Health & Chronic Ailments

                     Go from coping or crisis, to inspired & thriving!

Wisdom Ways Acupuncture helps women reach their highest level of well-being using Acupuncture, Chinese herbs, and Nutritional Guidance. With 20 years experience in both Western and Eastern clinics, Inger Giffin, L.Ac., Dipl. Ac. has helped transform the health of thousands of patients.

While some women come in for only small health corrections, many others arrive overwhelmed or afraid. Their body isn’t working right. They feel bad a lot of the time. Their reproductive system is completely out of balance or the aging process hasn’t been quite graceful.

If you know you don’t just want a pill to mask the problem but you’re at a loss for the next step, that’s where Inger comes in.

A big focus of her work of the past 20 years has been “educational medicine”. Through taking the time to make sure women understand their imbalances and giving them personalized tools, women leave not just feeling better than they’ve felt in years, but also inspired and empowered — having learned how to maintain health on their own outside the clinic as well as getting in touch with the incredible healing wisdom of their body.
