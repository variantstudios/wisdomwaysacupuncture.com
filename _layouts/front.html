Specializing in Acupuncture for Women's Health & Chronic Ailments

     Go from coping or crisis, to inspired & thriving!

If you’re struggling with chronic health issues, we can help. With 20 years experience in both Western and Eastern clinics, Inger has helped transform the health of thousands of women.

Maybe you need help with only small health corrections, or maybe you’re like many of our patients who arrive overwhelmed or afraid. Their body isn’t working right. They feel bad a lot of the time. Their reproductive system is completely out of balance or the aging process hasn’t been quite graceful.

If you know you can be living with better health but you’re at a loss for the next step, that’s where Inger comes in.

A big focus of her work of the past 20 years has been “educational medicine”: By putting patients in the driver’s seat, taking the time to make sure they understand their imbalances and giving them personalized tools; women leave not just feeling better than they’ve felt in years, but also inspired and empowered — having learned how to maintain health on their own outside the clinic. 

You deserve to know how good it feels to have a body that functions with vibrancy the way it was designed to do!
